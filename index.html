NASA Astrobiology Research Portal

NASA’s Astrobiology Program is dedicated to understanding the origin, evolution, distribution, and future of life in the universe. This interdisciplinary science integrates biology, chemistry, planetary science, astronomy, and geology.

Mission Overview

The Astrobiology Program investigates whether life exists beyond Earth, how habitable environments develop, and how biosignatures can be detected on planetary bodies such as Mars, Europa, Enceladus, Titan, and exoplanets orbiting distant stars.

Key Research Domains

  • Microbial survival in extreme terrestrial analog environments
  • Mars regolith chemistry and preserved biosignatures
  • Subsurface oceans on icy moons
  • Exoplanet atmospheric composition analysis
  • Organic molecule formation in interstellar space
